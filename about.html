About

The My Sister's Keeper Mobile App provides a platform for a global community of women and girls to receive loving afirmations about the questions they ask themselves growing up.

Not everyone has a positive maternal confident to help answer their questions and the App fills that void by allowing a young girl to ask a question about self image, relationships, or just know that someone loves them.

The Team

My Sister's Keeper is a program run by Divas for Social Justice based in Bedford Stuyvesant, Brooklyn, New York City, as part of the "Imaging Ourselves" series. E-mail info@divasforsocialjustice.org to find more about our programming for young women, or visit our web-site at http://www.divasforsocialjustice.org
